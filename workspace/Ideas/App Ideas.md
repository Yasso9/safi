## Evaluation Criteria

Before starting any project:
1. **Do I need this?** - Scratch your own itch first
2. **Does it exist?** - If yes, is it actually good?
3. **Can I maintain it?** - Long-term commitment
4. **Is it minimal?** - Complexity is the enemy

Most ideas should die in this file. That's okay.
